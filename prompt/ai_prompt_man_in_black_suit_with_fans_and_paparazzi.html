Celebrity-Style AI Prompt: Man in Black Suit Surrounded by Fans and Paparazzi

Back

How to use this prompt

Upload your photo as the reference so the man matches your face and look. Keep the black suit and sunglasses for the cool, celebrity vibe. The GoPro bird’s-eye angle and paparazzi flashes are key — don’t remove them. If you want to personalize, swap the crowd (fans → bodyguards, journalists → reporters) while keeping the dramatic angle for that “star moment” feel.

Prompt

Wide-angle GoPro-style shot from a high, symmetrical bird’s-eye perspective. \[Reference man from the photo] stands in black sunglasses and a sleek black suit, looking casually into the camera while holding his face with his index finger and thumb in a confident gesture. Around him, glamorous supermodels and enthusiastic fans reach out, touching his arms and shoulders, enhancing his magnetic presence. Below the frame, paparazzi flashes and crowded journalists with cameras create a chaotic yet dramatic atmosphere. The ultra-wide GoPro lens exaggerates perspective, making the scene feel immersive, dynamic, and larger-than-life like a raw, energetic capture from the middle of celebrity madness.

Why this prompt works

It combines fashion, chaos, and fame all in one frame. The GoPro angle makes the scene feel larger than life, while the fans and paparazzi add drama and energy. It’s not just a portrait — it tells a story of being the center of attention. That’s why this prompt creates eye-catching, scroll-stopping results.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “paparazzi” → “concert crowd” for a rockstar vibe.
  • Swap “black suit” → “white tuxedo” for a luxury red-carpet look.
  • Replace “GoPro angle” → “drone shot” for a more cinematic perspective.
  • Add “red carpet under his feet” to give it a Hollywood premiere setting.

FAQ

Do I have to use a GoPro-style wide angle?

Yes — that’s what makes the shot feel immersive and energetic. Without it, the scene loses the chaotic celebrity effect.

Can I replace the fans and paparazzi with something else?

Absolutely — you can swap them for models, security guards, reporters, or even a cheering sports crowd. The key is keeping the chaotic, attention-filled vibe.
