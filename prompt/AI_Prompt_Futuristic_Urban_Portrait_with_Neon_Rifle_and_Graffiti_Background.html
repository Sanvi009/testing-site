AI Prompt: Futuristic Urban Man with Neon Holographic Rifle

Back

How to use this prompt

Upload your photo reference to make the man look accurate. Keep the outfit and rifle details for consistency, since they define the futuristic style. You can experiment with the graffiti colors, light effects, or mural designs to change the mood while keeping the neon glow and comic-style background for that high-energy look.

Prompt

"Hyper-realistic portrait of a urban man (photo uploaded) wearing a green beanie, open army-green jacket, white t-shirt, brown cargo pants, and green slip-on shoes. He stands confidently holding a futuristic transparent holographic rifle glowing with neon blue and purple light. The background is filled with colorful comic-style pop-art murals with bold graffiti text, cosmic energy light effects, and radiant circular neon beams surrounding his body.The floor features comic patterns in vibrant colors, creating a modern, fantasy, high-energy atmosphere. Ultra-detailed, 8K resolution, epic futuristic style."

Why this prompt works

It mixes street culture with sci-fi. The outfit feels real and urban, while the holographic rifle and neon lighting add a futuristic twist. The graffiti murals and comic-style floor make the scene vibrant, energetic, and eye-catching. This kind of mix — realistic people in fantasy environments — creates images that feel unique and memorable.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “neon blue and purple” → “red and gold glow” for a fiery, powerful vibe.
  • Replace “comic-style murals” → “cyberpunk city streets” for a darker, more futuristic look.
  • Switch “holographic rifle” → “glowing energy sword” for a fantasy-warrior style.
  • Add “floating neon graffiti symbols in the air” for an even more surreal atmosphere.

FAQ

Can I change the outfit colors?

Yes — but keep at least one green item (beanie or jacket) if you want to stay close to the original urban-futuristic vibe.

Do I need to use the holographic rifle?

No, you can swap it for any futuristic weapon or tool (sword, staff, shield) — the neon glow and effects are what make it stand out.
