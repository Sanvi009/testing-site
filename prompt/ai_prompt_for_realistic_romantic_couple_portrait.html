Romantic Hyper-Realistic AI Portrait: Couple Embracing in Studio

Back

How to use this prompt

Upload reference photos for both the man and woman to keep their faces accurate. Keep the studio setup and cinematic lighting for a professional look. You can adjust clothing, background, or pose if needed, but keeping the close embrace and natural expressions ensures the image feels romantic and realistic.

Prompt

Hyper-realistic ultra-detailed 16K cinematic studio photograph of a man (same facial features as in the reference photo) holding a woman in his arms while kissing her. The woman wears denim shorts, a soft pink fitted top, and white sneakers. Shot in a professional studio with a seamless white background enhanced by dramatic cinematic lighting — soft key light with subtle shadows and a gentle backlight creating depth. Natural skin tones, lifelike expressions, highly detailed textures of fabric and hair. Magazine editorial style, crisp focus, shallow depth of field, high-end fashion photoshoot aesthetic.

Why this prompt works

This prompt works because it combines realistic faces, natural poses, and cinematic lighting. The studio background keeps the focus on the couple, while the details in clothing, hair, and skin make it look like a high-end fashion or editorial photograph. It’s perfect for realistic, romantic AI portraits that feel professional and intimate.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “denim shorts and pink top” → “evening dress and suit” for a formal look.
  • Swap “studio background” → “sunset beach” for an outdoor romantic vibe.
  • Adjust “kissing” → “holding hands and looking at each other” for a softer feel.
  • Change lighting → “golden hour natural light” for warm, dreamy portraits.

FAQ

Can I use this prompt for personal photos or just AI characters?

You can use it for both. Uploading reference photos makes it realistic and personalized.

Do I need a studio background?

No — you can replace it with any background, but a plain studio setup keeps the focus on the couple and ensures a professional look.
