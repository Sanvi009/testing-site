AI Prompt: Human Packaged Like Supermarket Food

Back

How to use this prompt

Upload a photo of the person you want to use. The AI will place them inside a supermarket-style fruit package with plastic wrap and a price sticker. You can edit the price tag text, barcode, or packaging color to customize the look, but keep the plastic wrap and condensation for maximum realism.

Prompt

A hyperrealistic surreal image of a human ("photo") packaged like supermarket fruit. The human is placed inside a red styrofoam container and tightly sealed with transparent plastic, with water droplets and condensation visible on the surface. A supermarket price sticker reading 'FACE CUT – PHP 355.99/K' with a barcode is attached to the forehead, with a serious expression. Dramatic dark background, cinematic lighting, tense and unsettling atmosphere, ultra-detailed, 8K photorealistic style.

Why this prompt works

It instantly grabs attention because it’s weird, disturbing, and realistic at the same time. The supermarket packaging makes it feel familiar, but putting a human inside it makes it unsettling and thought-provoking. That mix of normal and surreal is what makes people stop, stare, and share.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “red styrofoam tray” → “clear plastic container” for a more modern packaging look.
  • Replace “barcode sticker on forehead” → “label across the chest” for variety.
  • Switch “serious expression” → “blank expression” to change the mood.
  • Add “bright supermarket shelves in background” instead of dark background for a more commercial vibe.

FAQ

Is this meant to look creepy or funny?

It leans toward dark and unsettling, but you can lighten it up by changing the text on the price tag or adding a playful background.

Do I need to add the barcode and price sticker?

Not necessarily. The image works even without them, but the sticker makes it feel more like real supermarket packaging, which sells the surreal effect.
