How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it for character caricatures, animation concepts, or streetwear branding in fashion and media. Preserve "hyper-stylized 3D caricature," "modern character with exaggerated facial features," "urban streetwear," and incomplete prompt structure for confidence. Do not change the playful expression. Optionally add accessories; for example, "sunglasses" could yield cool visuals, resulting in oversized sneakers, while original creates confident, detailed outcomes.

Prompt

Create “Hyper-stylized 3D caricature of a modern character with exaggerated facial features, confident and playful expression. Wearing urban streetwear: hooded jacket, joggers, oversized sneakers.

Why this prompt works

This prompt is highly effective for creating a stylized and exaggerated 3D caricature that is both confident and playful the urban streetwear with oversized sneakers adds a modern trendy vibe while the exaggerated facial features ensure the character is memorable and engaging this style is perfect for social media avatars gaming characters or promotional content that targets a youthful audience looking for fun and identity.

  • Expression: Swap “confident and playful expression” → “a cool, aloof expression”. Swap “confident and playful” → “an angry, intense expression”.
